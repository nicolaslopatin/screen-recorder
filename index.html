Privacy Policy for Screen Recorder

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Screen Recorder handles information when you use the app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping data collection to a minimum.

1. Information We Collect

Screen Recorder is designed to record your device screen and save videos locally on your device. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

  • Screen recordings are created and stored locally on your device.
  • We do not upload your recordings to our servers.
  • We do not create user accounts.

2. Permissions Used

The app may request certain permissions only to provide core functionality:

  • Media and storage access: to save and access recorded videos on your device.
  • Foreground service and screen capture permissions: to enable active screen recording.

These permissions are used solely for recording functionality and are not used to collect personal data for advertising or tracking.

3. How We Use Information

Any data generated by the app (such as recorded video files) is used only to provide the recording feature requested by you. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.

4. Third-Party Services

The app itself does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that track personal data. If distributed through Google Play, Google may collect certain technical or diagnostic information according to its own policies.

You can review Google Play policies here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties because we do not collect personal data through the app.

6. Data Retention

Recordings remain on your device until you delete them. We do not store copies of your recordings on external servers.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the app secure. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children’s Privacy

Screen Recorder is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can delete recordings directly from your device whenever you choose.
  • You can revoke app permissions from your device settings.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: screen-recorder-controller@googlegroups.com
